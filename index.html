I am a Professor in the Department of Epidemiology and Biostatistics, College of Public Health, at the University of Georgia (UGA).

My research and teaching focus on data analysis and modeling of infectious diseases.

This website contains a short bio and products such as blog posts, presentations, and various tools and resources related to teaching and research.

More research related details can be found on my research group site. Some overlap between the two sites might occur.
